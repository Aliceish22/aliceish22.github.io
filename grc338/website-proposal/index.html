Introduction

Blooms

Blooms is a full-servie floral shop that specializes in fresh flower arrangments. We have a small team of made up of the founder, floral designers and customer service consultant. Blooms' main goal is to use locally sources flowers to create beautiful arrangments to brighten both homes and events.

Target audience

Our target audience is customers seeking to order arrangments in the local area. We want to attract a customer base that enjoys supporting local small businesses and growers. We're looking to serve those ordering arrangments for holidays, small events and for personal enjoyment.

We also want to focus on bringing in a younger demographic by using trendy plants and arrangments. These are you people looking to liven up their first living space with and interst in nature and sustainability. We'll feature planted succulent arrangments that can continue growing after purchase.
